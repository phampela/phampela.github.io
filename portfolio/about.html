I believe design is how thought becomes visible.

My practice sits at the intersection of image-making, writing, and digital product design. I came to design through photography — spending my early twenties shooting documentary work across southern Europe gave me an obsession with light, composition, and the stories that live in overlooked corners. Design followed naturally.

Over eight years working with studios, startups, and cultural institutions across Europe and beyond, I've learned that the most effective work comes from genuine curiosity. I ask a lot of questions. I read widely. I spend too much time in bookshops.

I currently take on a small number of product design and photography commissions each year. I'm also available for workshops and design mentoring through my studio practice.
